<ol>
<li>Click on the active field.</li>
<li>Write down a task.</li>
<li>Click on "add task" button.</li>
<li>The task will appear in the list below.</li>
<li>If you want to mark Your task as "done", please press green button.</li>
<li>If you want to delete Your task, please click on red button.</li>
